Chicken Curry

With the pop of spicy mustard seeds and heady fragrance of garam marsala, you can add as much chilli powder as you desire. Some Indian curries are made with a tangy tomato base and others on cream, yoghurt or coconut milk. This curry gives us the best of both worlds. Pieces of chicken are simmered in a spiced tomato sauce until tender and then finished off with rich and smooth coconut milk.
